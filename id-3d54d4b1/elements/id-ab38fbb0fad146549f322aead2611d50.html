<<eira:BindingInstrument>> Article 4b(2) - Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2021/2007 ()
The Implementing Regulation ensures that the information referred to in Article 37a(1) and Article 37b(2) and (3) of Regulation (EU) No 904/2010 is exchanged in a uniform manner, the Commission is to adopt practical arrangements for such exchange, including a common electronic message. Article 4 defines the transition of information between Member State of establishment and Member State of the exemption (bidirectionally).
