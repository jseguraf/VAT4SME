Validate the Notification ()
The call activity describes the validation of the Notification at the side of the MS of establishment. The MS of establishment has maximum 15 working days to perform these activities since within 15 working days from the notification, it must notify the MS of exemption. The following validations are performed: • syntactic validation; • semantic validation; • exceedance of the Union annual threshold. In case some of the validations are violated, the overall result from the validation will be negative.
