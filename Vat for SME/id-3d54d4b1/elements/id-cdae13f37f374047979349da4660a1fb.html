<<eira:LegalInteroperabilityAgreement>> COM (2005) 596 - Framework strategy for multilingualism ()
SOURCE: http://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=uriserv:c11084 http://europa.eu/pol/mult/index_en.htm The Commission's multilingualism policy has three aims: * to encourage language learning and promote linguistic diversity in society; * to promote a healthy multilingual economy; * to give citizens access to European Union legislation, procedures and information in their own languages.
