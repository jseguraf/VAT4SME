<<eira:PublicPolicyObjective>> Opens the VAT exemption to eligible SMEs not established in the MS where VAT is due ()
The new rules will open the exemption to small enterprises established in other Member States than the one in which the VAT is due. The exemption will apply if the turnover in Member State where the SME is not established is below the national threshold and if the annual turnover in the EU is below € 100,000. https://taxation-customs.ec.europa.eu/vat-scheme-small-businesses_en
