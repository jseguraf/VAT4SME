<<eira:Binding Instrument>> Regulation (EC) No 2560/2001 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 19 December 2001 on cross-border payments in euro ()
DEFINITION: This ABB is a Business Agnostic European Legal Act which lays down rules on cross-border payments in euro in order to ensure that charges for those payments are the same as those for payments in euro within a Member State.
